6/12

Basic Twitch Mod Commands

/timeout [username] [time]
Temporarily silence a user (1s-2w)
Formats: 30s, 5m, 1h, 1d, 2w (max 2 weeks)
/ban [username]
Permanently ban a user from chat
/warn [username] [reason]
Warn a user in chat
/emoteonly
Enable emoteonly mode
/clear
Clear the entire chat

Chatterino - Your Best Friend

Why use Chatterino?

  • ✨ Better chat visibility - See messages clearly
  • 🎨 Custom highlighting - Spot important messages
  • 🔧 Mod tools integration - Right-click to moderate
  • 📱 Multiple chats - Monitor several channels
  • 🚀 Faster performance - No browser lag
  • 🎯 User tracking - See user history quickly
📥 Download: chatterino.com - It's free and game-changing!

Mental Health as a Moderator

⚠️ Common Challenges You'll Face:

  • 😤 Dealing with angry/toxic users
  • 🤔 Making tough judgment calls
  • 😓 Feeling responsible for chat atmosphere
  • ⚖️ Balancing enforcement with fun
  • 😔 Receiving criticism from viewers
💚 Remember: You're human! It's okay to make mistakes and learn.
10/12

Healthy Coping Strategies

🎯 Stay Objective

Don't take toxic behavior personally - it's not about you!

💬 Communicate

Ask other mods or Zoil when you're unsure about decisions

⏸️ Take Breaks

Step away if you're feeling overwhelmed or frustrated

🎉 Focus on Positives

Remember the good moments and community wins!

You're part of a team - never moderate alone! 💪
